About ODBIT

ODBIT is a ROBLOX based organization that is meant to keep the community of ROBLOX safe, and to stop class action lawsuits. Our mission is to protect users from online predators, inappropriate content, and other threats in digital environments.

Founded with the goal of creating safer online spaces, ODBIT works closely with platform administrators, law enforcement agencies, and community members to identify and address violations of community guidelines and legal regulations.

Our Services

Contacting

We create responsive and quick ways to contact our managers via links to ensure immediate response to critical reports.

Staff

Our staff helps regulate the online community of ROBLOX, to keep people safe through active monitoring and intervention.

Reporting

We provide experienced people who have trained for ODBIT to report online sex crimes and online dating, ensuring prompt handling of serious violations.

Investigation

Our trained investigators examine reports thoroughly to ensure appropriate action is taken against policy violators.

Education

We provide resources to help users understand online safety and recognize potential threats in digital environments.

Community Support

Dedicated teams provide support to affected individuals and communities following incidents of online abuse.
